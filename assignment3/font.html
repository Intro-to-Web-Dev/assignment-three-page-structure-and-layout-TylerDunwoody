Font Demonstration

What fonts will I use for the body and h1-h6?

I chose this font that is called 'Mollen'. I like it because it is a very simple font. It has thin strokes so that it looks good especially in the news section.

What kind of weight and size am I using?

I didn't change any of the weights for the fonts. The size I use for the titles is 40 for layout one and 32 for layout two. The size of the information text is in the range of 13-16. These sizes depend on the style of box element they are in.

What is my desired line height?

The desired line height I have all depends on where it is. I want the text to fill up the whole box, but keep it so that the space is the same for each one.
