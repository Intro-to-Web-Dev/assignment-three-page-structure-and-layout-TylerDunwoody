Color Demonstration

How did I choose my color scheme?

I chose these colors because I wanted my website to feel light. My favorite colors are blue and purple so I chose a light version of each color. I also chose a deeper blue to contrast with the light blue I chose. I don't have any spots using this deeper blue yet but I plan to use it for some animation on the final project. I chose the cream becaus I think its more appealing than a normal white.
